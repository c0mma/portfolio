iPad App
for Museum of Childhood

Full case study

This exciting project was focused on the improvement of experience in the V&A Museum of Childhood in East London. This museum was founded in 1922 and has a big collection of toys and objects made for kids from different eras. As the majority of exhibits are quite rare and expensive, they are closed in glass stands inaccessible for visitors.

We had an opportunity here to make the Museum of Childhood more attractive and engaging for kids and their parents. Initial idea was to create a mobile/tablet application in order to improve the existing experience. But first things first.

Role
User Research, Prototyping, Visual Design
